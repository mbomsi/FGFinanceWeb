Membership Overview

Becoming a member of FG FINANCE means joining a trusted cooperative that puts people before profit. Members enjoy access to tailored financial solutions, dividend opportunities, and personalized services designed to help you grow financially and socially.

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Obtain a subcription form to be filled with information from your NIC with staff assistance

Step 2: Prepare Required Documents

Entrance fee = 2000 FCFA

Payment of Shares = 1500 FCFA

Building Fee = 5000 FCFA

Pay Solidarity fee of 5 000 FCFA

Pay Solidarity Fund of 40%(6000) of share value

Step 3: Pay Membership Fees

Pay a small registration fee and purchase minimum shares as defined by the cooperative’s policy. Details are available at any branch or agent location.

Step 4: Activate Your Account

Submit a photocopy of your NIC

Submit 2 (4*)photo for an individual account or 6 (4*4) for a group

Extract of group minutes, photocopies of NIC's and signatories for group accounts.

Step 5: Enjoy Member Benefits

Members enjoy dividend earnings, access to affordable credit, investment opportunities, and personalized financial guidance.
